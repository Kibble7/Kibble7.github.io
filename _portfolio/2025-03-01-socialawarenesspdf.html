" collection: portfolio --- # Social Engineering Awareness ## Introduction This document provides an overview of social engineering attacks, prevention strategies, and resources for phishing demonstration with practical examples. ## Social Engineering Attacks - **Phishing**: Fraudulent attempts to steal sensitive information via deceptive emails or websites. - **Pretexting**: Creating a false narrative to obtain personal or confidential information. - **Baiting**: Offering something enticing to get individuals to reveal their information or compromise security. ## Prevention - **Education**: Train individuals to recognize signs of social engineering attacks. - **Security Protocols**: Use strong authentication methods and verification procedures to prevent unauthorized access. ## Resources for Research Projects - **Social Awareness PDF**: [Social Awareness PDF](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1X1pbo_6NY_yBPaN1GkM7TRybd33jLOcd/view)
