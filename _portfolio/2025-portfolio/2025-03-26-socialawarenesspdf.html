Social Engineering Awareness

Introduction

This document provides an overview of social engineering attacks, prevention strategies, and resources for phishing demonstration with practical examples.

Social Engineering Attacks

  • Phishing: Fraudulent attempts to steal sensitive information via deceptive emails or websites.
  • Pretexting: Creating a false narrative to obtain personal or confidential information.
  • Baiting: Offering something enticing to get individuals to reveal their information or compromise security.

Prevention

  • Education: Train individuals to recognize signs of social engineering attacks.
  • Security Protocols: Use strong authentication methods and verification procedures to prevent unauthorized access.
