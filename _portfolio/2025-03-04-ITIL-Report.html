🛡️ Cybersecurity Audit Report: RMC Consultancy

🔍 Executive Summary

RMC Consultancy, a small financial services firm, experienced repeated cybersecurity breaches due to social engineering threats. Our audit identified gaps in employee training, incident response, and system change management.

📄 Download Full Report (PDF)

📌 Key Findings

  • 🚨 Employees lacked cybersecurity awareness training.
  • 📂 Poor documentation of system changes and updates.
  • 🔒 No multifactor authentication (MFA) implemented on critical accounts.
  • 🕵️ Inconsistent logging and monitoring of user activities.

🔧 Recommendations

  • 🎓 Implement mandatory phishing awareness and cyber hygiene training.
  • 🛠️ Enforce a change management policy with version control and peer review.
  • 🔐 Deploy MFA across all internal and cloud services.
  • 📈 Set up centralized logging and active monitoring with alerting capabilities.

📅 Implementation Plan

  1. Month 1: Begin employee training and policy rollout.
  2. Month 2: Apply MFA and basic logging improvements.
  3. Month 3–4: Full policy enforcement and advanced monitoring.
